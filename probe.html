In-house Prototype

This in-house prototype integrates moisture, humidity, temperature, and rainfall sensors to store real-time data accessible through this app.

Prototype Tools

Microcontroller

NodeMCU EPS8266 is an open-source firmware and development board that enables Wi-Fi connectivity, making it suitable for IoT projects by allowing easy control and data transmission to and from various devices.

Capacitive Soil Moisture

Capacitive Soil Moisture sensor measures soil moisture content by detecting changes in the capacitance of the sensor's probes as they interact with the surrounding soil.

DHT11 Sensor

DHT11 sensor is a digital temperature and humidity sensor that measures and provides real-time data on ambient temperature and relative humidity.

Rainfall Sensor

A rainfall sensor measures precipitation to determine the intensity and duration of rain. This rainfall sensor give value in binary format.

Solar Panel

Solar panels are photovoltaic devices that convert sunlight into electricity, providing a sustainable source of renewable energy.
